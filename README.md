# 🏥 Hospital Emergency Room Dashboard (Microsoft Excel)

## 📌 Project Overview
The Hospital Emergency Room Dashboard is an interactive Microsoft Excel dashboard designed to analyze emergency room patient data and monitor hospital performance. It helps healthcare administrators track patient flow, waiting time, admissions, referrals, and patient demographics through dynamic visualizations.

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze emergency room patient data.
- Monitor average patient waiting time.
- Track patient satisfaction.
- Compare admission and non-admission rates.
- Analyze patient demographics.
- Monitor department referrals.
- Support data-driven decision making.

---

## 📈 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- 👥 Total Patients
- ⏱ Average Wait Time
- ⭐ Patient Satisfaction Score
- 🏥 Admission Status
- ⏰ On-Time vs Delayed Attendance
- 👨 Male vs Female Patients

## 📊 Insights

- Total Patients: **479**
- Average Wait Time: **34.90 Minutes**
- Patient Satisfaction Score: **5.30**
- More than half of patients experienced delays.
- Nearly equal distribution of male and female patients.
- Most referrals were to General Practice.
- Adults (60–69 years) represented one of the largest patient groups.
